How can I get a market report?

A Split Lease market report gives you insider knowledge about available rentals, pricing trends, and personalized recommendations for your target area - completely free!

What's Included

Our market reports include current inventory, average pricing by neighborhood, availability patterns, and custom recommendations based on your specific needs.

How to Request a Market Report

Option 1: Email Request (Recommended)

Send an email to support@split.lease with:

  • Target city: Which city do you need a second home?
  • Preferred neighborhoods: Any specific areas you're interested in?
  • Your schedule: Which days and how often?
  • Budget range: What's your target per-night price?
  • Must-have amenities: Workspace, parking, pet-friendly, etc.

Quick Response

Our team typically responds within 24 hours with a customized market report. The more details you provide, the more tailored your report will be.

Option 2: Live Chat

Available on app.split.lease:

  • Click the chat icon in the bottom right
  • Ask for a market report for your target area
  • Provide your requirements in the conversation
  • Get instant guidance and follow-up via email

Option 3: Browse Directly

Prefer to research yourself?

  • Visit app.split.lease/search
  • Use filters to explore available inventory
  • View pricing across different neighborhoods
  • Save interesting listings to compare later

What's in a Market Report?

1. Current Inventory Overview

  • Number of available listings matching your criteria
  • Breakdown by neighborhood
  • Property types (studios, 1-beds, private rooms)
  • Availability for your specific days

2. Pricing Analysis

  • Average nightly rates by neighborhood
  • Price ranges from budget to premium
  • Estimated monthly costs based on your schedule
  • Comparison to hotels and traditional rentals

3. Neighborhood Insights

  • Commute times to major business districts
  • Neighborhood character and amenities
  • Public transit access
  • Popular areas among Split Lease guests

4. Personalized Recommendations

  • Top 3-5 listings matching your needs
  • Why each recommendation fits your requirements
  • Links to view detailed listings
  • Suggested next steps

Data-Driven Insights

Our reports are based on real-time platform data, showing you actual available inventory - not theoretical estimates. You'll see exactly what's available right now.

When Should You Request a Market Report?

You're new to the area
Don't know which neighborhoods to target? A market report helps you understand your options.

You're comparing costs
Need to justify the decision to your employer or family? Get concrete pricing data.

You're unsure about availability
Wondering if anyone offers your specific day schedule? We'll show you what's possible.

You want expert guidance
Our team knows the market and can point you to hidden gems you might miss on your own.

What Happens After You Get Your Report?

  1. Review the options: Read through the recommendations and pricing
  2. Ask questions: Reply with any clarifications or additional needs
  3. Schedule viewings: We can help coordinate trial nights
  4. Book your top choice: Start with a single night or week to test it out

No Obligation

Market reports are completely free with no commitment required. Use them to educate yourself, compare options, or plan for a future move.
