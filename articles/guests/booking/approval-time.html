How long does a host have to approve my booking request?

Understanding the approval timeline helps you plan your booking strategy and know what to expect after submitting a request.

Standard Approval Window

Hosts have 48 hours (2 days) to respond to your booking request. Most hosts respond within 24 hours.

The Approval Process Timeline

What Happens After You Submit

Minute 0: You submit your request

  • Host receives instant notification (email + app push)
  • Your request appears in their dashboard
  • The 48-hour countdown begins
  • You receive confirmation that your request was sent

Hour 0-24: Most responses happen here

  • 70% of hosts respond within the first 24 hours
  • Active hosts often respond within 2-6 hours
  • You'll get email/app notification when host responds

Hour 24-48: Reminder period

  • At 24 hours, host gets a reminder notification
  • Most remaining responses come in this window
  • You can send a polite follow-up message

Hour 48: Deadline

  • If host hasn't responded, request automatically expires
  • You're free to request other properties
  • No charge or penalty to you

Typical Response Times by Host Type

Host Type Average Response Time Notes
Super Hosts 1-4 hours Very responsive, experienced
Experienced Hosts 4-12 hours Check dashboard regularly
New Hosts 12-24 hours Still learning the platform
Occasional Hosts 24-48 hours May check less frequently

What Hosts Consider During Review

Understanding what hosts evaluate helps you create a strong request:

Your Profile Completeness

  • Profile photo: Real photo of you builds trust
  • Verification status: ID verification significantly increases approval rate
  • Payment method: Valid card on file shows you're serious
  • About section: Tell hosts about yourself and your needs

Your Message Quality

  • Personalization: Generic requests get lower priority
  • Clear dates: Specific schedule makes approval easier
  • Professional tone: Courteous, well-written messages
  • Relevant details: Why you need the space, your situation

Message Template That Works

"Hi [Host Name], I'm a [profession] who travels to [city] for work [frequency]. I love that your place is near [specific feature]. I'm clean, quiet, and respectful. Would love to book a trial night to see if it's a good fit!"

Schedule Compatibility

  • Does your schedule align with their available nights?
  • Are you flexible or rigid with dates?
  • Does your commitment length match their preference?

Guest Fit Assessment

  • Do you seem like a good match for their space?
  • Are you the type of guest they want to host?
  • Will you respect house rules?

Possible Outcomes

1. Instant Approval

Some listings have Instant Book enabled. You're automatically approved if you meet criteria:

  • Verified ID
  • Payment method on file
  • Positive reviews (for established guests)
  • Complete profile

2. Approved with Message

Host approves and responds with:

  • Welcome message
  • Additional questions or information
  • Instructions for next steps
  • Possible scheduling adjustments

3. Questions Before Approval

Host wants more information:

  • Answer promptly and thoroughly
  • Be transparent and honest
  • Show enthusiasm for the space
  • Demonstrate reliability

4. Declined

Host declines your request (happens rarely):

  • Dates no longer available
  • Found another guest
  • Schedule incompatibility
  • Don't take it personally - keep searching!

5. No Response (Expires)

Request expires after 48 hours:

  • Host may be inactive
  • May have missed notifications
  • Could be traveling without access
  • You're free to request other properties immediately

Pro Tips for Faster Approvals

Before You Request

1. Complete your profile 100%

  • Add clear profile photo
  • Get ID verified
  • Add payment method
  • Write detailed "About Me"

2. Read the full listing

  • Reference specific details in your message
  • Show you've done your homework
  • Demonstrates genuine interest

3. Request at optimal times

  • Best: Weekday mornings/afternoons (9am-5pm)
  • Good: Weekday evenings (6pm-9pm)
  • Slower: Late nights, early mornings
  • Slowest: Weekend nights

During the Waiting Period

1. Be patient but proactive

  • Wait at least 24 hours before following up
  • After 24 hours, send a friendly follow-up
  • Keep it light: "Just wanted to bump this up in case you missed it!"

2. Have backup options

  • Express interest in 2-3 properties simultaneously
  • You can't submit multiple binding requests, but you can message hosts
  • Once one approves, withdraw other requests promptly

Multiple Requests

You can only have one active booking request at a time. However, you can express interest or message multiple hosts before formally requesting.

3. Stay responsive

  • Turn on push notifications
  • Check email regularly
  • If host asks questions, respond within hours
  • Your response time matters to hosts too!

What If Host Doesn't Respond?

After 48 hours with no response:

  1. Request expires automatically - You won't be charged
  2. You're free to submit new requests - No waiting period
  3. Consider other listings - Responsive hosts make better partners
  4. Report persistent non-responders - Helps keep platform quality high

A host who doesn't respond within 48 hours may not be the most reliable choice anyway. Active, responsive hosts tend to provide better experiences.

Improving Your Approval Rate

Guests with 90%+ Approval Rates Do These Things

  • Complete profile with verification
  • Personalized, thoughtful messages
  • Start with trial nights
  • Build positive review history
  • Target appropriate listings (not overreaching)
  • Be flexible and accommodating

Special Circumstances

Urgent Bookings

Need a place quickly?

  • Mention urgency politely in your message
  • Look for hosts with "Quick Response" badge
  • Consider Instant Book properties
  • Message multiple hosts explaining timeline

Trial Nights

Trial night requests typically get faster approval:

  • Lower risk for hosts
  • Easier yes decision
  • Often approved in under 12 hours
  • Great way to build your profile

Returning Guests

Booking with a previous host?

  • Usually approved within hours
  • Established trust accelerates process
  • May not even need formal approval

Summary

Key Takeaways:

  • Hosts have 48 hours to respond
  • Most respond within 24 hours
  • Complete profile = faster approvals
  • Quality message matters
  • Trial nights get quicker responses
  • No response after 48 hours? Move on to other listings

Ready to submit your request? Make sure your profile is complete and craft a personal message. Good luck!
