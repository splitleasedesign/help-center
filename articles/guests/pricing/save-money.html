How are you saving me money?

Split Lease saves multi-local professionals thousands of dollars per year through smart pricing, no wasted rent, and eliminated fees. Here's the complete breakdown of how we make multi-city living affordable.

Average Savings

Split Lease users save $6,000-$15,000 per year compared to maintaining a second apartment or using Airbnb for regular travel.

1. Pay Only for Nights You Use

The fundamental difference:

Traditional rentals charge you for ALL 30 days per month, whether you're there or not. Split Lease lets you pay only for the nights you actually stay.

Real Example: Corporate Consultant

Maria travels to NYC Monday-Thursday (16 nights/month) for client work.

  • Traditional NYC rental: $2,800/month (paying for 30 days, using 16)
  • Split Lease: $95/night × 16 nights = $1,520/month
  • Monthly savings: $1,280
  • Annual savings: $15,360

2. No Airbnb Fees and Surcharges

Airbnb's hidden costs:

  • Service fee: 14% per booking
  • Cleaning fee: $75-150 per stay
  • Occupancy tax: 10-15% in most cities
  • Dynamic pricing: Rates spike on high-demand nights

Real Example: Airbnb vs Split Lease

Same apartment in San Francisco, 3 nights per week:

  • Airbnb listed rate: $150/night
  • + Service fee (14%): $21/night
  • + Cleaning fee: $40/night (amortized)
  • + Occupancy tax (14%): $21/night
  • Total per night: $232
  • Monthly cost (12 nights): $2,784

Split Lease: $110/night × 12 nights = $1,320/month

Monthly savings: $1,464 | Annual savings: $17,568

Bottom Line

For anyone who needs consistent accommodations in another city but doesn't use it full-time, Split Lease typically saves 50-70% compared to traditional options.

Calculate Your Savings

Quick formula:

  1. Count nights you'd use second city per month
  2. Multiply by average Split Lease rate ($80-120/night)
  3. Compare to full-time rent or Airbnb costs
  4. Calculate monthly difference × 12 months

Example:

  • You'd visit Boston 8 nights/month
  • 8 nights × $95/night = $760/month
  • Full Boston apartment = $2,400/month
  • Monthly savings: $1,640
  • Annual savings: $19,680
