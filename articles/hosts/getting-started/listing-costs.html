What are the costs of listing my place on your site?

Great news: listing on Split Lease is completely free. There are no upfront costs, monthly fees, or hidden charges. You only pay a small service fee when you actually receive bookings.

Zero Upfront Costs

$0 to create your listing. $0 monthly fees. You only pay when you earn.

Complete Cost Breakdown

To List Your Property: $0

  • Account creation: Free
  • Listing creation: Free
  • Photos and description: Free
  • Calendar management: Free
  • Guest messaging: Free
  • Profile unlimited listings: Free

While Your Listing is Active: $0

  • No monthly subscription
  • No listing fees
  • No marketing fees
  • No maintenance charges

When You Receive Bookings: Service Fee

Only pay when you earn:

  • Typically 10-15% of booking value
  • Deducted automatically from payout
  • Covers payment processing, customer support, platform maintenance
  • Much lower than Airbnb (20-25% total fees)
