What are the advantages to hosting the same guests part of the week?

Hosting recurring guests on Split Lease offers unique advantages you won't get with traditional rentals or Airbnb. Here's why hosts love the Split Lease model.

Key Advantage

Predictable income + less work + better guests = the Split Lease difference.

1. Predictable, Recurring Income

Unlike Airbnb's uncertainty:

  • Same guest books same nights every month
  • Automatic monthly payments
  • No scrambling to fill calendar
  • Forecast income 3-6 months ahead
  • Build financial stability

2. Much Less Work

Comparison:

  • Airbnb: Different guest every few days, constant cleaning, new instructions, reviews
  • Split Lease: One guest for months, occasional cleaning, one relationship
  • Time saved: 70-80% less hosting work

3. Better Quality Guests

Split Lease attracts:

  • Professional travelers
  • Employed, stable income
  • Mature, responsible adults
  • Treating it as their second home (respectful)
  • Long-term thinking (not party mode)

4. Lower Wear and Tear

Light usage guests:

  • Only there 8-16 nights/month
  • Often working during day
  • Treat space carefully (want to maintain relationship)
  • Less turnover = longer lifespan for furnishings

5. Tax Benefits

Periodic tenancy advantages:

  • May qualify as passive income (non-taxable in some cases)
  • Different tax treatment than STR
  • Consult tax pro for your situation
  • Learn more about tax benefits

6. Build Relationships

Benefits of knowing your guest:

  • Trust develops over time
  • Better communication
  • Flexibility on both sides
  • They become advocates for your property
  • Often renew for 6+ months

7. Less Regulation

Avoid STR restrictions:

  • No short-term rental license required (most cities)
  • Not subject to hotel taxes
  • Periodic tenancy = traditional rental laws
  • Fewer legal headaches

8. Use Your Space Too

Perfect for part-time residents:

  • Rent Mon-Wed, you use Thu-Sun
  • Both parties benefit from split schedule
  • Earn income on nights you're not there anyway
  • Keep your space for your own use

9. Pricing Simplicity

No dynamic pricing stress:

  • Set one monthly rate
  • No adjusting prices daily
  • No seasonal pricing complexity
  • Guests appreciate predictability too

10. Better Reviews & Reputation

Long-term guests = better reviews:

  • They know space well (fair reviews)
  • Appreciate the relationship
  • More likely to leave positive feedback
  • Builds your hosting credibility

Real Host Experiences
