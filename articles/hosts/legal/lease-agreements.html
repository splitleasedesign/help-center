How do lease agreements work on Split Lease?

Split Lease provides legally-compliant periodic tenancy agreements that protect both hosts and guests. Here's everything you need to know.

Automatic Agreement

Split Lease generates customized lease agreements automatically when a booking is confirmed. Both parties digitally sign.

What is a Periodic Tenancy?

Definition

A periodic tenancy is a rental agreement that continues for recurring periods (in Split Lease's case, specific scheduled days) with no fixed end date.

Split Lease Structure

  • Example: Guest rents Mon-Wed nights every week
  • Legal status: Full tenancy rights during booked periods
  • Flexibility: Guest only there certain days, host has space other times
  • Termination: Either party gives 30 days notice

Why Periodic Tenancy?

  • Provides guest with tenant protections
  • More stable than typical short-term rentals
  • Avoids many STR regulations
  • Better for guests making regular work commutes
  • Creates long-term relationship

Key Components of the Agreement

1. Parties

  • Landlord/Host: Your full legal name
  • Tenant/Guest: Guest's full legal name
  • Property address: Complete address of rental

2. Rental Schedule

  • Days of occupancy: Which days guest has access (e.g., "Monday 4pm - Thursday 10am each week")
  • Start date: When tenancy begins
  • Ongoing nature: Continues until terminated with notice

3. Rent Amount

  • Monthly rent: Total amount per month
  • Payment schedule: When payments are due
  • Payment method: How guest pays (through Split Lease)
  • Late fees: Penalties for late payment

4. Security Deposit

  • Amount (if any)
  • Held by Split Lease in escrow
  • Conditions for deductions
  • Timeline for return (14-30 days after tenancy ends)

5. Utilities and Services

  • What's included in rent (electric, water, WiFi, etc.)
  • What's not included (if anything)
  • Guest's usage expectations

6. Property Description

  • What guest is renting (private bedroom, shared common areas)
  • Square footage
  • Furnishings included
  • Parking (if included)
  • Storage space provided

7. House Rules

  • Smoking policy
  • Pet policy
  • Guest/visitor policy
  • Noise/quiet hours
  • Use of common areas
  • Cleaning responsibilities

8. Maintenance and Repairs

  • Host responsibilities: Major repairs, appliances, structure
  • Guest responsibilities: Keep clean, report issues, minor items
  • Emergency procedures: Who to contact

9. Access and Entry

  • Guest's right to exclusive use during booked days
  • Host's right to enter with notice (24-48 hours)
  • Emergency access provisions

10. Storage Rights

  • Guest may store personal belongings
  • Designated storage areas
  • Security of stored items
  • Liability disclaimer

11. Termination

  • Notice period: 30 days by either party
  • Early termination: Conditions and penalties
  • Move-out procedures: Cleaning, inspection, key return

12. Legal Provisions

  • Governing law (your state)
  • Dispute resolution (mediation, arbitration)
  • Entire agreement clause
  • Severability
  • Signatures and date

By host:

Dispute Resolution

Important Clauses Explained

Entire Agreement

The written agreement is the complete contract. Verbal promises not in writing aren't enforceable. Get everything in writing!

Severability

If one provision is invalid, the rest of the agreement remains in effect. Protects from entire agreement being voided.

Attorney's Fees

If dispute goes to court, prevailing party may recover legal fees. Encourages resolution before litigation.

Waiver

Allowing one violation doesn't mean you can't enforce rule later. Not enforcing once doesn't waive your rights.

Common Questions

Q: What if guest wants to change something in the agreement?
A: You can negotiate terms before signing. Once both parties agree and sign, it's final (unless you both agree to amend later).

Q: Can I use my own lease agreement instead?
A: Split Lease requires using platform agreement to ensure consistency and compliance. You can add custom terms to the standard agreement.

Q: What if guest violates the agreement?
A: Document violation, communicate with guest, use Split Lease dispute resolution. If serious, may need legal action.

Q: How do I terminate the agreement?
A: Either party provides 30 days written notice through Split Lease platform. Tenancy ends after notice period.
