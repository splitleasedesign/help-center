How do payments work? When do I get paid?

Split Lease handles all payment processing securely. You get paid automatically via direct deposit on a predictable schedule.

Automatic Payouts

Money deposited directly to your bank account. No invoicing, no chasing payments. Simple and reliable.

How Guest Payments Work

Guest Pays Split Lease

  • Guest pays through platform (credit card, debit, ACH)
  • Payment processed when booking confirmed
  • Money held securely by Split Lease
  • Released to you on payout schedule
  • You never handle payments directly

What Guest Pays

  • Rent: Your nightly/monthly rate
  • Service fee: Guest pays 3-5% to Split Lease
  • Cleaning fee: If you charge one (goes to you)
  • Security deposit: Held in escrow (returned to guest after stay)

Payout Schedule

Standard Schedule

24 hours after guest check-in:

  • Guest checks in on Monday
  • Payout initiated Tuesday
  • Money in your account Wednesday-Thursday (1-2 business days)

Why 24 Hours After Check-in?

  • Ensures guest actually checked in
  • No shows result in full refund (you don't lose money you haven't earned)
  • Protects both parties
  • Industry standard timing

Monthly Stays

  • First month: 24 hours after check-in
  • Subsequent months: 1st of each month
  • Example: Guest checks in Jan 15, you're paid Jan 16. February rent paid Feb 1, March rent paid Mar 1, etc.

Payout Methods

Direct Deposit (ACH)

Recommended method:

  • Directly to your bank account
  • Free (no fees)
  • 1-2 business days
  • Most reliable
  • Set up once, works automatically

How to Set Up

  1. Go to Settings → Payouts
  2. Add bank account details
  3. Verify with micro-deposits (2 small deposits, confirm amounts)
  4. Set as default payout method

Other Methods

  • Debit card: Instant payout available (1% fee)
  • PayPal: 2-3 day transfer (no fee for standard, 1% for instant)
  • Wire transfer: International hosts ($15 fee)

Service Fees

Host Service Fee

  • Standard: 10-15% of booking subtotal
  • Deducted automatically: Before you're paid
  • Covers: Payment processing, platform maintenance, customer support, insurance

Example calculation:

  • Guest books for $1,000/month
  • Your service fee: 12% ($120)
  • You receive: $880
  • Plus any cleaning fees (no fee on those)

Volume Discounts

  • $10,000+ annual earnings: 12% fee
  • $25,000+ annual earnings: 10% fee
  • $50,000+ annual earnings: 8% fee
  • Automatic - no action needed

Payment Issues

Guest Payment Fails

What happens:

  • Guest notified immediately
  • 24 hours to provide new payment method
  • Booking may cancel if not resolved
  • You're notified of the issue
  • No impact on you unless booking cancels

Your Payout Fails

Common reasons:

  • Bank account closed
  • Account number incorrect
  • Name mismatch
  • Bank rejected transfer

What to do:

  1. Check email notification
  2. Update payout method
  3. Verify all details correct
  4. Contact support if continues
  5. Payout will retry automatically

Disputed Charges

  • Guest disputes payment with bank
  • You're notified and asked for evidence
  • Provide booking details, communication, lease agreement
  • Split Lease handles dispute process
  • Most resolved in your favor if you have documentation

Taxes and Reporting

Tax Forms

  • 1099-K: If earn $600+ per year
  • Issued: January for previous year
  • Where: Download from dashboard

What's Reported

  • Total gross earnings (before fees)
  • Service fees ARE deductible expenses
  • Report net income on Schedule E

Keep Records

  • Export transaction history
  • Save all documentation
  • Track for tax purposes
  • Consult CPA for deductions

Security Deposits

How They Work

  • Guest pays deposit when booking
  • Held by Split Lease in escrow (not paid to you)
  • Released after guest's stay ends
  • You can claim deductions for damages

Claiming Deductions

  1. Document damage with photos
  2. Submit claim within 7 days of checkout
  3. Provide receipts/estimates
  4. Guest has 3 days to respond
  5. Split Lease mediates if disputed
  6. Approved amount sent to you, remainder to guest

Timeline

  • No claims: Deposit returned to guest 14 days after checkout
  • With claim: Resolved within 30 days

Cancellations and Refunds

Guest Cancels

Before check-in:

  • Your cancellation policy applies
  • May receive partial payment
  • Split Lease processes refund to guest
  • You're paid for non-refundable portion

After check-in:

  • 30 days notice typically required
  • You keep payment for notice period
  • Remaining rent refunded to guest

You Cancel

  • Before check-in: Guest fully refunded, penalties may apply to you
  • After check-in: Complex - contact support
  • Avoid canceling: Impacts your rating and future bookings

International Hosts

Currency

  • Listings priced in local currency (USD, EUR, etc.)
  • Guest pays in their currency
  • You receive in your currency
  • Exchange rate applied at time of payout

Payout Methods

  • International bank transfer (wire)
  • PayPal (many countries)
  • Local payment methods (varies by country)
  • Additional fees may apply

Tax Withholding

  • US hosts: No withholding
  • International hosts: May have 30% US withholding unless treaty
  • Complete W-8BEN form to claim treaty benefits
  • Consult tax professional

Common Questions

Q: Can I get paid faster?
A: Instant payout to debit card available (1% fee). Otherwise, 24 hours after check-in is standard.

Q: What if guest doesn't pay?
A: Guest can't book without payment. If payment fails later, booking may cancel and you keep cancellation policy amount.

Q: Can I accept payment outside Split Lease?
A: No, against terms of service. Removes all platform protections. Don't do it.

Q: How do I update my bank account?
A: Settings → Payouts → Add/update bank account. Verify with micro-deposits.

Q: Do I pay taxes on gross or net income?
A: Report gross, but service fees are deductible expenses. Consult CPA.
