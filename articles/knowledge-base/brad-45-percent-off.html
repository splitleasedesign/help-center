Brad gets 45% off Airbnb Price

Success Story

This is the story of how we helped Brad save 45% off his stay, a savings of $3,245 compared to Airbnb (with an Airbnb host!). Brad needed a place to stay for visitation with his daughter in NYC on the weekends. What Brad was looking for was a repeat rental.

Brad's Challenge

Before learning of Split Lease, Brad had pursued other solutions. Brad described his frustrated search for a "repeat listing for a return trip to the same location and the ability to see multiple non-continuous availabilities."

Brad Tried Lesser Alternatives:

1. Renting a Space Full-Time

While this could work, Brad would be required to pay for nights he isn't using the space making it very costly.

2. Booking a Hotel Room

While a hotel would allow Brad to pay for only the nights he needs, the room feels nothing like a home (no kitchen, check-in/check-out, single room, etc). Plus, Brad would be forced into paying a premium on the nightly price that comes with hotels.

3. Using Craigslist

Brad resorted to Craigslist to try and piece together his own solution with someone who could accommodate. In his search, Brad had come across a Split Lease host that advertised on Craigslist as well.

The Split Lease Solution

Brad needed a space safe enough for him and his young daughter to return to. He knew the price he was willing to pay to achieve his goals and negotiated with many hosts, ultimately creating a positive relationship with Nneka.

What Made Split Lease Unique

Brad was happy to tell us how helpful we were. Our, "ability to show repeated visits to the same place over a long period of time with the regular frequency was unique and valuable and then my only extra need then was an ability to select a longer return frequency."
